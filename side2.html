Salamaders

  • The salamanders HomeWorld is Nocturne and it's moon prometheus.
  • The salamanders warcry is Into the fires of battle, unto the Anvil of War.
  • The Salamanders are one of the Loyalist First Founding Chapters of the Space Marines. They originally served as the Imperium's XVIIIth Space Marine Legion during the Great Crusade and the Horus Heresy. For some time before their unification with their primarch they were known as the "Dragon Warriors.
